About Me

OID is a company I started during the Covid lockdown. With so much time on my hands, I revisited my first love that I had never pursued, as there was not much information in my time after high school besides the most popular white-collar careers. But I always knew I had a deep love for putting colours and elements together. That season opened up an opportunity for me to research around my natural gifting and I studied online to obtain knowledge around the technicalities of interior design. I have not looked back since‚ÄîI build on that every day and keep myself up to date with current styles and designs.

More than just decorating, I'm about transforming spaces into cohesive and beautiful environments that inspire and delight. Based in Randburg, I'm Caroline Tabi, and I'm here to guide you through the exciting journey of interior design.

What I Offer

From concept to completion, I design cozy, functional, and aesthetically pleasing interiors that reflect your personality and style.

I specialize in creating homely spaces for renters and homeowners, bringing warmth, elegance, and comfort into every project.

Whether it's a compact apartment or a luxury suite, I tailor every detail to suit your needs and budget.

Initial Consultation and Conceptualization

  • Client Interviews: Understanding the client's needs, lifestyle, preferences, budget, and goals for the space.
  • Site Analysis: Evaluating the existing space, including dimensions, layout, natural light, and architectural features.
  • Concept Development: Creating initial concepts, mood boards, and sketches.
  • Space Planning: Developing optimized floor plans and layouts.
  • Budget Development: Establishing a realistic project budget.
2

Design Development

  • Material and Finish Selection: Choosing suitable textures, colors, tiles, and finishes.
  • Furniture and Accessories: Sourcing furniture, curtains, lighting, artwork, and decor.
  • Color Palette: Creating a cohesive mood-driven scheme.
  • 3D Renderings: Providing realistic visuals of the proposed design.
3

Project Management and Execution

  • Coordination with Contractors and Vendors: Collaborating with architects, builders, contractors, and suppliers to ensure the design is executed according to the plans and specifications.
  • Procurement: Managing the purchasing, ordering, and tracking of furniture, materials, and accessories.
  • Installation Supervision: Overseeing the installation of furniture, fixtures, and finishes to ensure quality and adherence to the design.
  • Site Visits and Inspections: Conducting regular site visits to monitor progress, address any issues, and ensure the design intent is being met.
  • Budget Management: Tracking expenses and ensuring the project stays within the agreed-upon budget.
  • Timeline Management: Developing and adhering to a project¬†schedule.
